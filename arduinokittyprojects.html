Up above you can see what the arduino tv looks like, I used a 16x2 lcd screen an IR reciever and an arduino. in the case that the image did not load here's the wireing. vo = potentiometer vss = ground vdd = posotive rs = digital 12 rw = ground IR signal = digital 9 en = 11 d4 = 5 d5 = 4 d6 = 3 d7 = 2 A = posotive (through 220 ohm resistor). TIPS; Make sure you use a 220 ohm resistor because any more resistence and the images will not show and any less you lcd might burn out; change which digital pins you use to fit your needs; use a large breadboard; Change the IR remote buttons for you remote!
