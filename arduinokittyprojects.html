He's catching a mouse! So, this is the part where everyone falls asleep while I explain how I made it. I used a 16x2 lcd screen an IR reciever and an arduino to make the thing work, the arduino as the brains the IR receiver as the ears and the lcd as the muscle. In the case that the image did not load here's the wireing. vo = potentiometer vss = ground vdd = posotive rs = digital 12 rw = ground IR signal = digital 9 en = 11 d4 = 5 d5 = 4 d6 = 3 d7 = 2 A = posotive (through 220 ohm resistor). TIPS; Make sure you use a 220 ohm resistor because any more resistence and the images will not show and any less you lcd might burn out; change which digital pins you use to fit your needs; use a large breadboard; Change the IR remote buttons for you remote! I will go into how lcd screens display images another time!
